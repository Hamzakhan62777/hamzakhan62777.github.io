Privacy Policy for "Turn"

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

Welcome to Turn. Your privacy is important to us at Lansivo. This policy explains how information is handled within our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect or store any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. Any game progress, such as your survival time and high scores, is stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Unity Ads)

To provide the game for free, we use Unity Ads for monetization. Unity may collect certain non-personal device information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device model and operating system
  • Advertising ID (IDFA/AAID)
  • IP Address and location data

You can view Unity's full privacy practices here: Unity Privacy Policy.

3. Children's Privacy

Our game is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.
